About

Ever since we can remember, we were fascinated by different cuisines and flavors. When we finally started to establish my own restaurant , we discovered how much joy we get from watching others enjoy my restaurant's dishes. From healthy quick bites to an impressive meal for your friends and family, we're here to provide you the best foods, drinks and servies to our customers. Browse my site for a short tour of our restaurant and don't forget to check out our specials. We started our business in 2000 with one goal in mind: providing an enjoyable dining experience to the Ghattekulo area. Thanks to our experience and dedication, we've managed to become masters of the craft. Providing dishes that are fresh, healthy and simply unforgettable.
